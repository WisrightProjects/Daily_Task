FOOD MENU

Biriyani

Biryani is a timeless celebration of flavor, aroma, and tradition—one of the most beloved dishes across the Indian subcontinent. Made with fragrant long-grain basmati rice, tender cuts of meat or fresh vegetables, and a rich blend of spices, biryani is slow-cooked to perfection, allowing every ingredient to infuse deeply into the dish.

Honey Chicken

Honey Chicken is a perfect blend of sweet, savory, and lightly crispy goodness—an irresistible favorite for food lovers of all ages. Tender pieces of chicken are coated in a light batter, fried to golden perfection, and then tossed in a rich, glossy honey-based sauce that delivers just the right touch of sweetness.

Noodels

Noodles are a comforting and versatile favorite, loved for their satisfying texture and endless flavor possibilities. Made from wheat, rice, or other grains, they are cooked to perfection and tossed with a vibrant mix of vegetables, sauces, and spices to create a dish that’s both simple and delicious.
